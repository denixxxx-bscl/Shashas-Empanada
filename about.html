ABOUT US

Our Story

Shasha’s Empanada began from something very personal. The name “Shasha” comes from Trisha, a nickname lovingly given to me by my family. What started as a simple name soon became a dream I wanted to share with others through food. On September 28, 2025, I officially started this small business with one goal in mind: to bring the authentic taste of Ilocos empanada to Manila.

When you choose Shasha’s Empanada, you’re not just buying food — you’re supporting a small dream and a passion for sharing comfort through every bite.
